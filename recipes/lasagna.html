How to make Lasagna: a guide.

Description

Lasagna is a typical Italian dish wich has the purpose of connecting a family altogether. It's taste is its power, it's strenght, and why it's still a classic in modern cooking.

If you want to learn how to cook lasagna, you're at the perfect place, because i'll tell you everything you need to know to make the tastiest lasagna and impress you friends !

Ingredients

You'll need:
  • Tomato sauce
  • White sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Some Lasagna's pasta
  • Onions
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Parsley
  • And of course: Mozarella Cheese !

Steps

  1. Put some cooking oil in a pan
  2. When the oil is hot enough, add the ground beef to the pan
  3. Season with some salt and pepper
  4. When it's cooked, add your tomato sauce and some parsley
  5. Stir for quite some time until everything is mixed and cooked together
  6. Add a first layer of Pasta to a big plate
  7. Add on top some of the beef and tomato sauce and equalize before adding the white sauce
  8. Repeat until the plate is filled with your ingredients
  9. For the last layer, instead of adding white sauce, put your mozarella cheese generously on top !

  11. Serve hot and eat happily !
