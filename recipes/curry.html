How to make Chicken Curry and Rice: a guide.

Description

Student and tired of always eating your same pasta with cheese ? Here's the perfect recipe for you!

Warning, this dish even if heavily inspired by Indian cuisine isn't following any official recipe, it's just made from very common ingredients that you could find anywhere.

Ingredients

You'll need:
  • Cream
  • Curry Powder
  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Salt & Pepper
  • (Cayenne Pepper: Optional)

Steps

  1. Wash a volume of rice, had 1.5 to 2 volume of water in the rice cooker, add your washed rice along with some salt and put it to cook!
  2. Dice your chicken and season it with salt, pepper, curry powder, some cayenne pepper if you have it and oil.
  3. Heat up your pan, when it seems hot you can add your chicken in the pan and wait for all the faces to be well colorized.
  4. Add your diced bacon to the frying pan and stir for it not to burn
  5. When the chicken since almost cooked but still quite raw in the inside, you can add your cream to make a beautiful curry sauce !
  6. Add some more curry powder in it if you think it's too light and let it reduce for a bit.
  7. When the rice is cooked, serve it hot in a plate, add on top of it your chicken curry with some very prettty golden sauce
  8. Add some pepper and herbs on top
  9. Bon appetit!
